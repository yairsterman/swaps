How Does Swaps Work?
Swaps allows you to easily and securely exchange homes with people all over the world. Start swapping today and save a ton of money on your travels!

With Swaps, you can easily enter information about your home, the dates on which you plan to travel, and the destination where you want to stay during your trip. Swaps then finds and connects you with Swappers from different cities who have entered parallel information, so you can enjoy a home exchange specifically tailored to your needs.

As part of the swap, Swappers are required to set aside a security deposit to cover for possible property damages or cancelations. But don’t worry, Swaps will return the entire security deposit to you at the end of your trip, provided that no cancellations took place and no damage claims were filed. In fact, the security deposit never even leaves your bank account, so you can rest easy.

Click here for more information about the security deposit. Swaps has no annual membership fees or complicated points systems - you pay a tiny amount, and only for actual nights swapped. For the price of a cheap cup of coffee, Swaps will find you a wonderful home wherever you go, cover for potential damages and unexpected cancellations, protect you from shady users, and give you the peace of mind you need when you travel.
