What is Swaps all about?
Swaps is about creating a home swapping solution for a new generation of digital nomads around the world. Our platform offers wanderers at heart an, easy, safe, and cheap way to live their dreams and travel anywhere, hassle-free.

It’s easy - You stay at their home, they stay at yours. With Swaps, you don’t have to worry about your home while you travel, and you don’t end up blowing your budget on cookie-cutter hotel rooms. Even if you travel only a few days each year, Swaps will save you literally hundreds of dollars, ensure your home and personal belongings are secure while your away, and provide you with a convenient, safe, and comfortable place to stay wherever you go. Swaps makes you a local anywhere, and lets you make the most of what traveling has to offer.


In order to assure our users and their homes are safe during travels, Swaps asks that you set aside a security deposit to cover for possible property damages or cancellations. The entire sum of the deposit is returned to you after your trip. In fact, the security deposit never even leaves your bank account, so you can rest easy. (How can this be? Click here for more information about the security deposit.)

Swaps has no annual membership fees, hidden charges, or complicated points systems - you only pay for actual nights swapped. For a ridiculously low price, Swaps will give you the peace of mind you need in order to live life with no excuses and travel the world with no regrets.
